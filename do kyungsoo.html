Do Kyungsoo

Do Kyungsoo, also known by his stage name D.O., is a South Korean singer,songwriter, actor, and member of the popular boy band EXO. He was born on January 12, 1993 in Goyang, South Korea. He has been active since 2012 as a member of EXO.

He is famous for his soulful and emotional singing, and has been praised for his acting skills in various dramas and films. He made his acting debut in the drama 'It's Okay, That's Love' in 2014, and has since appeared in several other popular dramas such as '100 Days My Prince' and 'Room No. 7'.

Kyungsoo's talent and hard work have earned him a dedicated fan base both in Korea and around the world. I am also one of his fan who love to listen his songs. His voice has power which can touch the heart of every listener.
